Description

Idli is a popular, soft, spongy South Indian steamed savory cake made from a fermented batter of rice and black lentils (urad dal), known for being light, nutritious, vegan, and easily digestible, typically served hot with sambar and chutney as a breakfast or snack.

The fermentation process enhances flavor and nutrients, resulting in a mildly tangy taste and fluffy texture after steaming in molds.

  • Idli rice
